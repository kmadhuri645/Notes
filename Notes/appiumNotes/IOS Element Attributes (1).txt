IOS Element Attributes
In case of IOS "type" is Tagname and in case of Android "class" is tagname
 Example of xpath in IOS = //type[@Attributename='Attributevalue']
eg.1. XCUIElementTypeStaticText  for Text
   2. XCUIElementTypeButton for button
in IOS value attribute holds text for UI element  label attribute is also imp for static text
